  • The Team

    We are Ace Racing, a team of six students from The English School of Nicosia with a burning passion for Design & Technology, Physics, and Mathematics, participating in this year's 'Formula One in Schools' international competion; after achieving 2nd place in the national finals and winning the 'Fastest Car' award.

  • The Ace

    The Ace conveys our excellence in our areas of expertise, and the fact that we are a single entity - a team. We are comprised of students from both communities, and we aim to set an example to the island and the whole world that anything is possible regardless of borders and cultural differences.

  • The Competition

    The competition is organized annually by 'Formula One in Schools Ltd'. Even though Cyprus is new on the stage, our school has introduced and pioneered the program in Cyprus, and leads as an example to the rest of the island's schools. The competition calls for innovative ideas, critical thinking and enterpreneurship, which inspires us to expand our knowledge on the fields of project management, physics, aerodynamics, design, scoial media, and most importantly, teamwork. This year, Cyprus will be having its own national finals and we are excited to compete. If our team qualifies, we will be representing our island in the world finals in Malaysia later this year.
