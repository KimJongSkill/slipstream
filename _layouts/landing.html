The STEM challenge

F1 in Schools is the world's largest STEM competition, with over 20 million students from over 45 countries competing in teams, tasked with designing and manufacturing a scaled model F1 car as well as creating and managing many other project elements such as portfolios, a pit display, sponsorship and their team identity.
