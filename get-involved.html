In order for us to continue our journey and fulfil our dreams for Cyprus, we will need your support in order to cover the costs of the competition, and in return, we will provide excellent promotion for your company.

This will be done through our social media accounts - which have many followers from Cyprus and other participating countries such as Germany, Italy, Japan and USA - local and international news agencies, TV and radio interviews, which will be devoted to our sponsors and team, our website and attendance in any large local events. We stress that the promotion will not only target audiences within Cyprus, but globally, as the competition will be watched live by thousands on YouTube and on the Sky Sports channel. In addition to that, we would like to inform you that the World Finals event will be taking place at the same time as the Singapore Grand Prix, which hopefully we will attend. This will again be another exceptional opportunity for us to promote all our sponsors in an event which will be watched globally by an enormous amount of people.

Proceeds from all sponsorships will be used exclusively for the purposes of the competition, including participation fees, building material, transportation costs, manufacturing services, and team clothing. Sponsorships can also be in the form of goods and services relevant to the team's needs, and can be negotiated and allocated accordingly.
